Introduction to Drop Shadows

Shadows are a natural part of our world and help us perceive distance and position and a lot of information about the objects around us. Just like in the real world the digital world shadows help us visualize and position UI elements.

Drop Shadows are often used to emphasis parts of a website or used to show elevation. Material Design guidelines go over how to use drop shadows for elevation in depth. On this site I will be going over some different uses of drop shadow that are a-typical but can help with web accessibility.
